Egg Salad

Great for any sandwich

Ingredients

  • 8 Eggs hardboiled
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 rib celery
  • 2 teaspoons fresh dill

Steps

  1. Cut eggs in half remove yolks and chop whites
  2. Mash yolks with mayo mustard and salt and pepper to taste
  3. add remaining ingredients and stir well
  4. Put on sandwich
