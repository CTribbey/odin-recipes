Sloppy Joe

A great sandwich to bring back the memories of your mothers cooking

Ingredients

  • 1 LB lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup ketchup, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef 3-4 minutes then add onion and bell pepper, continue until vegetables are soft and beef is cooked through
  2. Stir in ketchup, brown sugar, mustard, and garlic powder; season with salt and pepper
  3. Slap on some buns and eat
