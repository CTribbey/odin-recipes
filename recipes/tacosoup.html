Taco Soup

Taco soup is a great one pot meal to fix anytime of the year. It can feed many and is delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 can Kidney beans
  • 1 can Corn
  • 1 can Rotel
  • 6 cups of Water
  • 6 chicken bullion cubes
  • 1 LB ground beef

Steps

  1. Brown beef in skillet
  2. Dump everything into a pot or crock-pot
  3. Cook until desired taste
